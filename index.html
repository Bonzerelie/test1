Welcome to the Ear Training Lab!

The games, quizzes, videos and content in this app are designed specifically to develop practical and purposeful musical listening skills, which compliment your overall musicianship in a way that reinforces understanding of related theory - particularly for modern pop, rock, jazz, soul and indie music, whilst also being useful for traditional classical music and other genres.

They are created by a working gigging music professional and teacher to cover beginner to professional level skills.
