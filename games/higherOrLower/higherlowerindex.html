How To Play

You'll hear two notes played back-to-back. Decide if the second note is Higher, Lower, or the Same as the first. This trains the core skill of relative pitch — comparing two notes and detecting the direction of movement.

Use the answer buttons or the arrow keys: ⬆️ Higher, ⬇️ Lower, ➡️ Same. Press R to replay the notes and Space / Enter to advance to the next note.

Change the note range at any time via Settings ⚙️. A wider range makes the game harder. Scores with 5+ answers are automatically saved to your Progress Tracker.
